ABOUT PINNACLE

Founded in 2025, Pinnacle Talent Management was created to support the next generation of creators, athletes, and digital personalities navigating the rapidly evolving digital landscape.

As the worlds of sports, social media, and entertainment continue to merge, emerging talent requires representation that understands both the creative and business sides of building a lasting career. Pinnacle works closely with its clients to build their brands, expand their audiences, and unlock new opportunities across multiple platforms.

Today, Pinnacle represents a growing roster of talent across boxing, digital media, podcasting, and entertainment, helping them maximize their potential through strategic partnerships, brand development, and innovative content strategies.

"Our mission is to elevate talent beyond the trends and build sustainable careers that transcend platforms."
